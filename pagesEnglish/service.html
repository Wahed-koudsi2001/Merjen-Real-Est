Our Best Legal Services

Litigation Services

Our firm specializes in providing legal representation for clients in commercial and civil disputes. Our team of experienced litigators is dedicated to advocating for our clients and achieving favorable outcomes in court. We offer comprehensive litigation services, including case assessment, strategy development, negotiation, alternative dispute resolution, and trial representation. Our goal is to provide effective and efficient legal services that meet the unique needs of our clients and help them achieve their objectives.

Arbitration Services

Our firm offers arbitration as an effective and efficient alternative to court litigation for resolving disputes. Our neutral and qualified legal experts ensure fairness and justice in arbitration proceedings. Our arbitration services include preparing arbitration agreements and terms, forming arbitration panels, receiving claims and demands, and issuing final and legally binding decisions in accordance with local and international arbitration laws. Our arbitration service is one of the most important services we offer to our clients and aims to provide effective and sustainable legal solutions for commercial and civil disputes.

Document Certification

Our firm provides document certification services to our valued clients, including attesting powers of attorney, executing real estate deeds, mortgage and lien documents, and authenticating company incorporation documents. Our certified notary publics authorized by the Ministry of Justice provide accurate, efficient, and reliable document certification services. Our goal is to ensure that our clients' documents are properly certified and authenticated in accordance with local and international laws and regulations.

Inheritance Division

Our firm provides inheritance division services in accordance with Islamic Law, which regulates the distribution of the deceased's estate among the heirs. Our team of experienced lawyers specializes in handling inheritance cases, ensuring that the division process is carried out fairly and efficiently. We provide comprehensive services, including identifying the heirs, calculating their shares, and drafting the necessary documents. Our goal is to help our clients navigate the complex legal procedures and ensure that their rights are protected throughout the inheritance division process.

Legal and Sharia Consultancy

Guided by the ethical principles of our profession and the teachings of Islam, our team of legal and Sharia experts are committed to delivering distinguished legal and Sharia advice tailored to meet each client's unique needs. We take pride in our accumulated expertise and dedicated team who work diligently to help our clients make informed decisions and avoid legal risks. Our aim is to provide our clients with the best possible guidance while upholding the principles of our religion and the laws of the land.

Regulations and Contracts Drafting and Review Services

At Faisal Al Faozan & Partners Law Firm, we are dedicated to achieving the highest level of accuracy, clarity, and consistency when drafting and reviewing regulations and contracts. Our team of experienced lawyers and legal experts work tirelessly to ensure that all regulations and contracts are in compliance with Islamic law, as well as official regulations and legislation, to ensure their continuity and preserve the rights of all parties involved. Leveraging our accumulated expertise and direct experience, we strive to minimize discrepancies and disputes by identifying potential issues and providing tailored solutions. Our goal is to provide our clients with comprehensive legal support in all aspects of regulations and contract drafting, review, and negotiation.

Company Formation Services

At Faisal Al Faozan & Partners Law Firm, we offer a range of services related to company formation in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our services include the formation of commercial companies with Saudi, foreign, or mixed capital, as well as obtaining the necessary licenses from relevant authorities such as the Ministry of Investment for various sectors including the service, commercial, industrial, and real estate sectors. We provide our clients with comprehensive support throughout the company formation process, from initial planning to final implementation, to ensure that all legal requirements are met and that our clients' interests are protected.

Corporate Legal Services

At Faisal Al Faozan & Partners Law Firm, we are committed to providing high-quality legal services to businesses and corporations, covering all legal aspects needed by the company, from establishment procedures to professional representation before third parties, and legal representation in all types of litigation. We offer a variety of flexible options for long-term contracts or specific services, providing tailored solutions to meet the unique needs of each client. Our services also include corporate governance, which involves regulating the relationship among shareholders, the board of directors, executive management, and stakeholders, as well as defining authorities and committees based on the principles adopted by the Saudi Arabian Monetary Authority and the Companies Law. These efforts aim "to build trust among shareholders, attract investors, clarify the obligations of all parties involved, and achieve justice, transparency, and improved performance to reach the desired goals.

Intellectual Property Protection

As an accredited agent of the Saudi Authority for Intellectual Property, we provide registration services for all types of intellectual properties, such as trademarks, industrial designs, copyrights, and others. In addition, we handle the preventive aspect by drafting and preparing contracts related to property rights, as well as filing objections to their registration from the initial stages up to the judicial stages, in accordance with the provisions of the Trademark Law, the Copyright Law, the Patent Law, the Copyright Protection System, and other relevant regulations in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.

Corporate Governance

We regulate the governance of joint-stock and other companies by controlling the relationship between shareholders, the Board of Directors, the executive management, and stakeholders, defining powers and committees based on the foundations approved by the Capital Market Authority in the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, and the Companies Law. This ensures shareholder confidence, attracts investors, defines the obligations of the parties concerned in the company, achieves more justice and transparency, and raises the level of performance to achieve the desired goals.
