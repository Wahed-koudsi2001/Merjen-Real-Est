Real estate development

The process of improving land or buildings through planning, design, construction, and marketing with the aim of increasing their value. This includes residential, commercial, or industrial projects, and aims to meet market needs and generate financial returns for investors.

Real estate marketing

Promoting properties to attract buyers or renters. This involves using strategies such as advertising, social media, and on-site demonstrations to highlight the property's benefits and increase the chances of selling or renting it.

Real estate supervision

Managing and following up on property maintenance and operation processes to ensure quality and continuity of services. This includes supervising maintenance, managing tenants, and monitoring compliance with laws and standards.
